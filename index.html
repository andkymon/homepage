JOSEPH JOESTAR

ABOUT ME

If there’s one thing you should know about me, I always have a plan! …Okay, maybe not always, but I’ll still outsmart you in the end! I’m quick on my feet, great with my fists, and even better at ticking people off. Oh, and don’t bother trying to predict my moves, because your next line is... 'Wow, this guy’s incredible!'

MY SKILLS

RIPPLE OVERDRIVE

It’s real simple: I channel the Ripple into my limbs or even an object, then bam!—it’s like a lightning-charged smack that wrecks whatever it hits!

CLACKER VOLLEY

I charge up these clackers, hide them behind my back, then whip them out in all directions. Good luck dodging that!

TOMMY GUN

Nothing says surprise attack like a Tommy gun! I emptied an entire magazine into Straizo, giving him no time to react. And just when he thought that was it, I followed up with a Ripple-charged strike.

PILOTING

I know how to fly a plane but let’s just say landings aren’t my specialty. I’ve crashed four times, but hey, I walked away from all of them, didn’t I? If anything, that just proves I’m great at surviving planes, not just flying them!
